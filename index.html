RECTANGULAR STEP SCREEN

WASTEWATER TREATMENT PLANT DESIGN

Kushal R. Purkayastha

Step screens are essential components in the initial treatment stage of wastewater treatment, which is a critical step in protecting the environment and public health. By using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to construct an interactive web-based calculator for the design and optimization of step screens in wastewater treatment facilities, this small project presents a novel solution. By providing a user-friendly platform, the main goal is to streamline the step screen design process. An interactive calculator that can calculate the best length, width, and height based on user-input parameters like flow rate, approach velocity, and head loss requirements is created by the embedded HTML, JavaScript, and CSS codes. This initiative makes a substantial contribution to wastewater treatment sustainability in addition to being a technological achievement and helps to safeguard our environment. The web-based calculator streamlines and optimizes the step screen design process, doing away with the need for laborious manual calculations, lowering the possibility of design errors, and guaranteeing the effective use of step screens.
